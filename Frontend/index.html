About Us

The algorithm visualizer was initially designed as a way to help us understand our own messy code. Turns out, we still don't get it, but now it looks pretty!

We're a team of three engineering students, surviving on caffeine, last-minute cramming, and the dream that one day we might actually sleep before a deadline. At Visco, we break down complex algorithms into simple, digestible visuals – perfect for those of us who prefer clarity over chaos (and maybe even a nap). As future engineers, we’ve mastered the art of studying just enough to pass, but here, we go all in to make coding understandable, one visual at a time. Forget the jargon – let’s make this fun and easy!
